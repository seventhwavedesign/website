A brand new course!

Facilitated by:

Matthew Davis

As Founding Director of 3C Consult Create Communicate, Matthew Davis has worked with, among others, Save the Children, Comic Relief, BBC Media Action, Scope, the Marine Conservation Society, EDF Energy, South Downs National Park Authority, and Friends of the Earth. He is also a guest lecturer in environmental communications at Birkbeck University.

Before setting up 3C Matthew worked at the World Wildlife Fund (WWF) for eight years, as Climate Change and Marine Campaigns Director, Communications Director for WWF's global species programme and Head of Press & Campaigns.

This training will help you understand how to keep your collaborators happy and engage them in a way that is effective, innovative and inspiring. Every organisation has responsibilities to those who make you who you are. These could be your supporters, your funders, your board, your staff or your trustees.

To ensure that you keep receiving and building on the support network you have created it is crucial that you continue to develop effective and enduring stakeholder relationships - and this course will teach you how.

You will learn:
Increase participation in your organisation, campaign or cause
Identify, prioritise, and interact with your key players
Assess the key issues relating to engagement
Build capacity through your supporters
How to expand and retain your community of stakeholders
Develop an engagement strategy
How to work with your participants on a change agenda

Who should attend the training?
This day is perfect for any organisation that is looking to build and strengthen its relationship with their stakeholders. It is great for local authorities working within the community who are trying to bridge the gap between service users and providers; charities looking to develop partnerships and their supporter base; and local groups wanting more engagement with their communities.
